Live match centre

What powers the broadcast-style Live page during a game.

Score, clock, lineups & timelineLive
FIFA — official feed ↗
FIFA's official match feed is the spine of the Live page: real-time score and match clock, starting XIs and formations, and a goal / card / substitution timeline with pitch positions — plus official player headshots. Read straight from FIFA in your browser while a match is on (no key, no proxy).
Commentary, box score & oddsLive
ESPN ↗
Play-by-play text commentary, an expanded box score, and pre-match odds (used only as a win-probability prior) come from ESPN's public soccer feed, alongside team colours and logos.
Advanced stats — xG, momentum, shot mapLive · best-effort
SofaScore ↗
When reachable, SofaScore adds real expected-goals per shot, an attack-momentum curve, and a full statistics table. If it's unavailable, the page falls back to an on-device model (always labelled "est.") so nothing breaks.
Scores, schedule & standings

The Fixtures, Groups and Bracket pages.

Scores, fixtures & kickoff timesLive
wc2026api.com ↗
All 104 matches with scores, clock/phase and status. Refreshed when you open a page and about every 75 seconds while a match is live. Calls run through this site's own proxy, which caches responses and caps usage at 490/day to protect the API key.
Group standingsLive · computed
wc2026api.com ↗
The API gives group membership; the tables are computed in-app from match results, so points, goal difference and qualification spots update as games finish.
Golden boot & scorersDaily
wc2026api.com ↗
The tournament top-scorers leaderboard is rebuilt once a day from match-event timelines — current to the last refresh rather than ticking live.
Players
Squads, market values, bios & photosSnapshot
Transfermarkt ↗
~1,250 players across all 48 squads — positions, clubs, ages, market values, portraits and profile links. Captured once before the tournament via the Apify Transfermarkt scraper.
Name pronunciationsSnapshot
Forvo ↗
Tap-to-hear audio for country names and select players, recorded by native speakers (used with attribution).
Teams & countries
Country profilesSnapshot
REST Countries ↗
Capital, population, languages, currencies, neighbours and time zones — assembled from the open mledoze/countries dataset plus World Bank population figures.
Badges, stadium photos & socialsSnapshot
TheSportsDB ↗
Team crests, venue imagery and social links across team pages (47 of 48 teams). Also the fallback for player photos when a Transfermarkt portrait is missing.
FlagsBuilt-in
flag-icons ↗
Open-source SVG flags served straight from this site — no external calls.
Rankings & records
FIFA World RankingOfficial
FIFA ↗
The official ranking, frozen at the pre-tournament edition — FIFA doesn't re-rank during a World Cup.
Elo ratingsSnapshot
eloratings.net ↗
A predictive strength rating shown next to the official ranking for contrast (via a public Kaggle dataset).
Head-to-head, all-time & WC historySnapshot
openfootball & Kaggle ↗
Past meetings between every pair of teams and all-time win/draw/loss records (international results since 1872), plus previous World Cup results.
